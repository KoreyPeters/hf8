Welcome to Polium

Polium is a game you can play in real life that encourages politicians to enact policies which meet the goals of HF and human flourishing.

The Polium gameplay loop

The concepts of Polium are similar to all HF games.

  1. Politicians are registered with the Polium system.
  2. HF players evaluate each politician according to HF's criteria, giving the politician a rating for how much they can be expected to promote human flourishing.
  3. HF players are rewarded for voting for politicians with high HF ratings.

Get Involved

Polium also gives its players points for performing other activities that help bring about human flourishing.

The full list of Polium activities is as follows:

  1. Rate a politician. This is done from the politicians page.
  2. Intent to vote. This can be from the election page by anyone with legal standing to vote in that election. It is a commitment to vote for the candidate whose Polium rating is highest.
  3. Voted. This is final proof that you voted in the election for the candidate whose rating was highest at the time of the election.

Other activities may be added as the platform matures.
